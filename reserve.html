  • Seating

    There is the option to book for COUNTER or TABLE seating when making your reservation. All availability will be shown for each section.

  • Group Bookings

    Bookings for 6-10 guests are considered a group booking and incur a 10% service fee on the total bill. Unfortunately, we do not have a Private Dining Room and we are unable to accommodate groups larger than 10 people.

  • Dietary Requirments

    Please let us know in advance if you have any dietary requirements as our kitchen will need to design a menu especially for you.

  • Availability

    Reservations are available up to 60 days in advance. If your desired date or time is unavailable, you can request to be notified if there is a cancellation.

  • Cancellation Policy

    A valid credit card is required to secure your booking. All no-shows or bookings cancelled with less than 24 hours’ notice will be charged a $200pp cancellation free. If you are feeling unwell, please call us on (02) 9567 9169 and we are happy to reschedule your reservation without any penalty charged.
